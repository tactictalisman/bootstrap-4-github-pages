##### Weathering
- Dark Wash across the entire surface.
- Light Chip coat sponged on using 4 drops Olive Drab, 3 drops white, 1 drop water.
- Dark Chip coat painted on with a fine brush using 4 drops grey, 1 drop brown, 1 drop olive drab, 2 drops water.
- Used Track Wash on the exhaust.
- Used a Light Rust wash in a few places.
- Used streaking grime on a few panels.
